About Monster Survivors

Monster Survivors is an intense survival game where you face endless waves of terrifying monsters. As the last survivor in a post-apocalyptic world, you must use your skills, weapons, and environment to stay alive as long as possible.

With simple controls but deep gameplay mechanics, Monster Survivors offers hours of challenging fun. Unlock new characters, upgrade your abilities, and compete with players worldwide on the leaderboards.

The game features stunning graphics, smooth controls, and an immersive soundtrack that will keep you on the edge of your seat.

How to Play

1. Choose Your Character

Select from various survivors, each with unique abilities and playstyles.

2. Survive the Waves

Fight off increasingly difficult waves of monsters using your weapons and skills.

3. Upgrade & Evolve

Collect experience points to level up and unlock powerful upgrades.
